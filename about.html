About Me

Jack Cornish (he/him)

I make Animations, Artworks and FX! I love to work in 2D and 3D formats but I'm usually working in 3D. I have experience in TVC's and Games.

I graduated from RMIT's Bachelor of Design (Animation) in 2021. I'm currently a casual lecturer and freelancer.

Houdini is my main piece of software which i use for Character FX, Rendering, Motion Design, Simulations, Procedural Assets, probably some more.

I animate in 3D with Blender and Maya, and in 2D with AnimDessin and After Effects.

Software in proficiency order: Houdini, Blender, After Effects, Maya, Nuke. Vray & Redshift.



I'm based in Richmond, Victoria, Australia.
